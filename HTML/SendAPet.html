Finding new home for your pet?

If you decide to sending your pet, bear in mind that, despite shelters and rescues' best efforts to care for your animals, your home is always the best place for your pet while you look for an adoptive.

When it comes to sending a pet to our shelter, we follow very strict guidelines. We only have a limited number of pets that we can accommodate, so we're only able to help those animals that are in extreme pain and have no one to care for. We can only rescue animals in an emergency if there are volunteers and transportation available and if someone can temporarily foster the animal until space becomes available.
